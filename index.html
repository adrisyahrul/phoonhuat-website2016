COURSES

Wilton Method of Cake Decorating

Welcome to Wilton Method of Cake Decorating Courses. All courses are conducted by certified WMI instructors endorsed by Wilton USA.

Wilton Cake Decorating Method Class are split into four courses which can be viewed below.

  • Phoon Huat Wilton Course is now claimable from SkillsFuture. Find out more
  • All material will be provided for class use only.
Course 1 : Building Buttercream Skills

Build Buttercream Skills you can use for all decorating projects - from cupcakes to cakes for any occasion. Whether you're new to decorating or have been decorating for years, when you complete Course 1, you'll be proud of the new techniques you've learned and ready to showcase them on your next project.

For Beginner Book Online
Course 2 : Flower and Cake Design

You'll learn core techniques to make a wide variety of flowers, such us pansies, lilies and The Wilton Rose™ using royal icing, the ideal medium for creating decorations in advance.You'll also learn how to design a professional-looking cake, from selecting the color to arranging your flowers.

Require Course 1 Book Online
Course 3 : Fondant and Gum Paste

Develop new skills working with fondant and gum paste, and apply them to create shapes, borders and flowers. You'll also learn how to cover a cake with fondant. Plus you'll design and create a complete project using all the techniques you were taught.

Require Course 1 Book Online
Project Course : Advanced Stringwork

In this Advanced Stringwork Project Course, you will learn how to specialized techniques into simple steps that will bring your cake to the next level. Creating drop String Designs, Double Drop String, Triple Drop String, Vertical Drop String and Borders.

Require Course 1 & 2 Book Online
Project Course : Wired Gum Paste Flowers

You will learn how to make the Rose, Hydrangea and Hibiscus with Gum Paste.

Require Course 1 & 3 Book Online
Project Course : More Wired Gum Paste Flowers

In this project course, you will learn how to make the Dogwood, Phalaenopsis (Orchid), Stephanotis and Tiger Lily with Gum Paste

Require Course 1 & 3 Book Online
